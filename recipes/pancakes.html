Pancakes

Description

These pancakes are easy to make, super fluffy, they look and taste amazing. You can have them with all the syrups and toppings you want.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp white sugar
  • 2 tbsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tbsp salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 large egg, beaten

Steps

  1. Combine flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a large bowl. Make a well in the centre, pour in milk, oil and egg, mix till smooth
  2. Heat a lightly oiled frying pan over medium high heat. Pour batter onto the griddle, 1/4 cup for each pancake; cook until bubbles form and the edges are dry (1-2 mins), flip and cook until browned on oth sides. Repeat this process with all of the remaining batter.
  3. Serve the pancakes hot and enjoy, add the syrup and toppings you want, I like maple syrup or honey, and strawberries.

Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Description

The crunch and taste of a grilled cheese sandwich is the perfect thing in the morning, I've also added some meat for flavour.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices bread
  • 2 cups grated cheddar cheese
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 slices dry-cured spanish chorizo
  • 2 slices salami

Steps

  1. Butter one side of each bread. Heat a pan to medium heat, and place one slice butter face-down.
  2. On top, add your cheddar cheese, chorizo, and salami to the bread.
  3. Place the other slice of bread on top, butter-side up. After about 2 minutes, flip the sandwich.
  4. Let it cook until it gets a nice golden-brown on both sides and the cheese has melted to your desired consistency.
  5. Slice it in half diagonally, serve hot, and enjoy.

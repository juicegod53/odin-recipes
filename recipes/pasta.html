Shrimp Scampi with Pasta

Description

Delicious large shrimp in a buttery, garlicky, white-wine sauce, coated with lemon juice, and a variety of herbs. This is combined with a delicious pasta that is boiled to perfection, this recipe uses linguine but anything works.

Ingredients

  • 16oz linguine pasta
  • 4 tbsp butter
  • 5 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 shallots (diced)
  • 2 garlic cloves (minced)
  • Pinch of kosher salt and ground pepper
  • 1/2 cup white wine
  • Lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup parsley leaves (chopped)

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil, cook the linguine until nearly tender (6-8 mins)
  2. Melt 2 tablespoons butter with 2 tablespoons olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat
  3. Cook and stir shallots, garlic, and red pepper in the hot butter and oil until shallots are translucent (3-4 mins)
  4. Season shrimp with kosher salt and black pepper, add shrimp to the skillet, cook until pink, stirring occasionally for 2-3 mins
  5. Remove the shrimp from the skillet and keep the warm. Pour white wine and lemon juice into the skillet and bring to a boil
  6. Melt 2 tablespoons butter in the skillet, stir 2 tablespooons olive oil in, and bring to a simmer
  7. Add the linguine, shrimp, and parlsey into the butter mixture, season with salt and black pepper
  8. Drizzle with 1 teaspoon olive oil to serve, and enjoy it while hot
